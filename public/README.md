# Portfolio Assets

## Images

### Profile Photo
- Add your professional photo here as `profile.jpg`
- Recommended size: 400x400px or larger (square aspect ratio)
- Format: JPG or PNG
- Update the Hero component (`src/components/Hero.jsx`) to use your image

### Project Screenshots
- Add screenshots of your projects
- Name them descriptively (e.g., `ecommerce-project.png`)
- Reference them in the Projects component

## CV/Resume

- Add your CV/Resume as `cv.pdf` in the `/public` folder
- The download button in the Contact section will automatically link to it

## Icons & Favicons

- Replace `vite.svg` with your own favicon
- You can generate favicons at https://favicon.io/
